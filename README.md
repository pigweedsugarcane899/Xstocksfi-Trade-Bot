## 📊 Strategy Overview

### Strategy 1: Trend Follow

This strategy watches for price movement in one direction. It tries to join a move after the trend is clear. This works best when the market shows a steady rise or fall.

### Strategy 2: Breakout Watch

This strategy looks for price to move past a key level. It can help when the market stays still for a while and then starts to move fast.

### Strategy 3: Reversal Check

This strategy looks for signs that a move may be ending. It can help when price stretches too far in one direction and may turn back.
